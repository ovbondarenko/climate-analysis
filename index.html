Summary: Hawaii weather in November


The goal of the project is to analyse historical climate data of the Hawaiian islands recorded and predict future wather for a selected range of dates. We like to travel to Hawaii on a Thanksgiving week (November 22 - December 1, 2019) and want to know what the weather will be like during this time of the year and which island is likely to have the warmest and dryest weather.

The precipitation and temperature observations are provided for the years of 2014-2017 by 9 different weather stations located on Hawaiian islands. We analyse the dataset to see which weather stations have been more active, what are their geographical location and visualize the recorded historical data for the last 12 months and the days of your planned trip.
